Description of user group

Our web app is for anyone who is looking for good barbers near them. Whenever I move to a new neighborhood, it takes me quite a while to find myself a good barber who knows my hair and cuts it as I want it to. Yelp has reviews of entire barber shops but never individual barbers; our app will provide galleries of individual barbers that can be filtered by their individual strengths.

Assigned Projects

There are three main pages for this website: map view of barber locations, list view of different barbers, and profile pages of individual barbers. In the prototyping stage, Stephen created the map page, Adela created the list page, and Yasmeen created the individual profile pages.

Additional instructions

On the map page, users will be able to see the exact locations of barbers nearby. They should be able to click on the locations to see the profile pages of individual barbers. On the list page, users will be able to filter barbers according to their specific strenghts and rank them in order of distance, ratings, or prices. Each preview of barber on the list will also lead to the profile pages.
